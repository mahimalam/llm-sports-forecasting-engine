I think France will arrive in 2026 as one of the absolute favorites. Their blend of youthful exuberance and seasoned talent is formidable. With Mbappé leading the line and players like Dembélé providing width, I expect their attack to be devastatingly potent, especially in transition. The midfield, anchored by Tchouaméni and the dynamic Camavinga, offers both defensive steel and creative spark, with Griezmann pulling the strings. Defensively, I see a solid unit, though they can sometimes switch off, which is something Didier Deschamps will need to address. However, the sheer quality of individual defenders like Saliba and Upamecano, combined with Maignan's shot-stopping, should provide a strong foundation. I anticipate them progressing deep into the tournament.
