I think Ecuador enters World Cup 2026 as a team that will be incredibly difficult to play against, especially with their robust midfield. I expect their athleticism and relentless pressing game to cause significant problems for many opponents. The core of Caicedo, Hincapié, and Estupiñán provides a really solid spine for the team. They are well-drilled defensively and transition quickly from defense to attack. My main concern, however, lies in consistent goalscoring. While they possess exciting wide players like Plata and Páez, I think finding a truly prolific center forward will be crucial for them to progress deep into the tournament. Their physical approach could also lead to disciplinary issues against highly technical opponents. Overall, I expect them to be a tough side to break down, capable of snatching results, particularly through rapid counter-attacks. Their progression will likely hinge on unlocking more offensive consistency.
