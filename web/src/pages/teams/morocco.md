I think Morocco will once again be a formidable force in 2026. Their strength truly lies in the balance they strike between a resilient defensive structure and explosive attacking transitions. I expect the core of their midfield, with the likes of Amrabat and Ounahi, to control games and link play seamlessly, allowing their dynamic wingers and full-backs, particularly Achraf Hakimi, to drive forward. What I've observed is their ability to absorb pressure and then break at pace, which can trouble any opponent. However, I believe they might still face challenges when asked to consistently dominate possession and break down teams that sit deep. I also anticipate continued reliance on moments of individual brilliance, and they'll need their strikers to be clinical on the big stage if they want to repeat or surpass their previous success. Overall, I expect a well-drilled, passionate side.
