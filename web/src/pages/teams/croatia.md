I think Croatia will enter the 2026 World Cup with a compelling blend of seasoned campaigners and exciting young talent. I expect their traditional strength to lie undeniably in the midfield, where Marcelo Brozović, Mateo Kovačić, and Lovro Majer will dictate tempo and retain possession with customary precision. Their ability to control the game's rhythm remains paramount to their system. Defensively, Joško Gvardiol is a genuine colossus, but I feel the overall unit, particularly against pacy counter-attacks, might still show some vulnerabilities. Upfront, the search for a consistently prolific goalscorer continues. While Petar Musa offers a physical presence, I anticipate Croatia might still struggle for prolific finishing. Their tactical flexibility and fierce team spirit, however, will make them a challenging opponent for any team they face.
