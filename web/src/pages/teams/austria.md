I think Austria under Ralf Rangnick will arrive at the World Cup 2026 as a highly organized and relentlessly pressing unit. Their defensive structure, led by players like Danso and Lienhart, is incredibly solid, and I expect them to be very difficult to break down. The midfield, with the energy of Laimer and the creativity of Sabitzer, provides the engine room for their dynamic transitions. I believe their ability to win the ball high up the pitch and break quickly will be their primary offensive weapon. However, I think they sometimes lack a truly prolific goalscorer, which could be a concern in tight games. While Baumgartner and Wimmer offer direct threats, converting chances consistently will be vital. Overall, I expect them to be a dark horse capable of surprising bigger nations with their collective work ethic and tactical discipline.
