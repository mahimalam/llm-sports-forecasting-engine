I think Canada will approach the 2026 World Cup with a clear identity, leveraging their athletic prowess and dynamic wide play. However, I believe their biggest challenge will be depth and consistency in front of goal, especially if Jonathan David is not firing. While they possess moments of brilliance, sustained creativity and clinical finishing across multiple games will be key to progressing beyond the group stage. I foresee them as a challenging opponent, capable of causing upsets on their home soil.
