I think this USMNT squad has a fantastic blend of youthful energy and growing experience, particularly with a home World Cup. The 4-3-3 formation perfectly suits their dynamic midfield, allowing Tyler Adams to dictate play while McKennie and Musah provide tireless running and ball progression. On the wings, I expect Pulisic and Weah to be constant threats, using their pace and directness to unbalance defenses. Folarin Balogun offers a genuine goal threat, something often lacking in previous campaigns. Defensively, I expect their high press to be a key weapon, making them difficult to play against. However, I think they might be vulnerable to quick counter-attacks, and consistency in clinical finishing could be an issue in tight knockout games. Overall, I expect them to leverage their home advantage and athleticism to make a deep run.
