Panama will likely adopt a pragmatic, defensive approach, leveraging their 5-4-1 formation to ensure solidity. I think their strength lies in their exceptional defensive organization and physical presence, particularly in midfield and the central defense. I expect them to frustrate opponents, sitting deep and limiting space aggressively. I don't think they'll dominate possession, but rather look to hit opponents swiftly on the break. Capitalizing on set-pieces, with José Fajardo’s aerial ability being absolutely crucial, will also be vital. I expect their ability to convert limited chances will be the determining factor in any potential success.
