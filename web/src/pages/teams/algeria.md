I think Algeria will approach World Cup 2026 with a pragmatic yet exciting style, built on a very solid defensive foundation. I expect their backline and midfield pivot, anchored by Bennacer, to be extremely difficult to break down. They'll look to absorb pressure and then transition quickly, utilizing the pace and individual flair of players like Mahrez and Benrahma on the wings. Their creativity in wide areas is a significant asset, and I believe it’s where most of their attacking threats will originate. However, I sense a potential challenge in converting chances consistently, particularly from central areas. While they boast significant talent, I expect them to rely heavily on moments of individual magic to unlock stubborn defenses, rather than intricate, flowing team moves. They'll be a tough opponent for any team in their group.
