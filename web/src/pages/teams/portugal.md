I think Portugal's 2026 squad, featuring a blend of experienced stars and rising talents, will be a formidable force. The 4-3-3 formation allows them to leverage their incredible midfield creativity with Bruno Fernandes orchestrating, supported by Vitinha's dynamism and João Neves's control. I expect their attacking trident of Leão, Ramos, and Bernardo Silva to cause significant problems for any defense, offering pace, trickery, and goal threat. Defensively, Rúben Dias anchors a strong backline, complemented by attacking full-backs like Nuno Mendes and Dalot. However, I think their tactical discipline will be crucial; they sometimes struggle to maintain defensive solidity against elite opposition. Their success will hinge on converting individual brilliance into consistent team performances and managing big game pressure.
