I think Spain arrives at the 2026 World Cup with a potent blend of youthful exuberance and midfield mastery. The core of Rodri, Pedri, and Gavi will provide unparalleled control and creativity, allowing the dynamic wingers, particularly Lamine Yamal, to thrive in attacking transitions. I expect their possession-based game to dominate opponents, wearing them down before unleashing quick, incisive attacks. However, I believe they must improve their clinical finishing against resilient defenses. The attacking full-backs provide width, but I anticipate this could leave them slightly exposed to rapid counter-attacks, a potential area for concern. Overall, a formidable squad capable of a deep run.
