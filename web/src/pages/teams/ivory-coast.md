I think Ivory Coast will approach the 2026 World Cup with significant confidence, building on their AFCON success. I expect their 4-3-3 formation to be highly dynamic, leveraging the athleticism of Franck Kessié and Ibrahim Sangaré in midfield to dominate the center. Defensively, Evan Ndicka and Odilon Kossounou offer a solid foundation, capable of both physical challenges and intelligent ball distribution. I anticipate their transition play will be a key weapon, turning defense into attack quickly. However, maintaining tactical discipline throughout a tournament could be a challenge, and depth in certain areas might be tested. Overall, they're a team with the potential to surprise.
